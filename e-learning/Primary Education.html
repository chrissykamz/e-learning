We think Education



At we think education we work closely with primary school teachers and pupils to create hands-on, interactive activities and resources that add to the curriculum experience, while also promoting the idea that educational progression can be achievable, accessible and desirable. Our work introduces younger children to the theme and vocabulary of higher education, inspires them by bringing university knowledge to life, and allows them to meet current university students and staff to order to excite them about future possibilities. Through this work, we are forging strong links with local communities and presenting real opportunities for primary schools to actively engage with higher education and its resources.
